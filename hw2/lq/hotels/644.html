        Welcome to the La Quinta Inn & Suites Gonzales hotel conveniently located on Highway 183 near popular local attractions just as Gonzales Memorial Museum, Old Jail Museum and The Eggleston House landmark. Nearby Pioneer Village Living History Center is five miles away and features landmarks from the 1800s, including log cabins, a blacksmith shop, and a number of historic houses. Palmetto State Park is less than 10 miles away and offers recreational activities like fishing, swimming, hiking and picnicking. Spoetzl Brewery, home of Shiner Beers, is in nearby Shiner, Texas and offers free brewery tours.

        Our staff at La Quinta Inn & Suites Gonzales strives to make your stay convenient and stress-free with our many amenities. Stay connected from the comfort of your room with free high-speed internet access. Our guest rooms our equipped with microwaves, refrigerators, 32" flat screen TVs, Pillow-top mattresses, coffee makers, hair dryers, iron and boards, and free local calls. Our meeting facilities make this a great place for your next meeting, baby shower or any special events.

        As a guest of the La Quinta Inn & Suites Gonzales hotel stop by and enjoy the complimentary Bright Side Breakfast. Wake up on the bright side and start your day off right with fresh coffee, tea or hot chocolate, assorted juices, pastries, breakfast breads, hot & cold cereals, hard boiled eggs, a fresh assortment of fruits and waffles. Relax by our outdoor swimming pool with hot tub. Keep up your exercise routine at our fitness center and take care of business at our business center. You can also stop by our Bright Side Market if you forget any of your essential amenities.
